ABOUT

We work with a goal-oriented objective to plant and really focus on trees and assist individuals with diminishing their carbon impression while at the same time creating business choices for provincial ranchers.

PROJECT

THE ISSUE

Because of deforestation, the woodland front of India has fallen underneath the base suggested level. As per specialists, woods should cover around 33% of the absolute zone of country. However, in India backwoods covers around 24% of the absolute territory. Satellite pictures show that India has acquired a woodland territory of 5,871 square kilometer during the time frame somewhere in the range of 2010 and 2012.
